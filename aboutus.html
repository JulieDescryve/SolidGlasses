Our story

Our story started 2 weeks ago, we noticed that most glasses ended up in the back of the drawer or in the the trash. Our idea is to be able to recycle them and sell them to you at a lower cost. All this by respecting the environment and your wallet

Our mission

Our aim is to create a global awareness regarding the issue of overconsumption through a stylish product that everyone can wear with prid.

Our customers

Our consumers care about the environment and expect sustainable use of resources, reduced emissions and greater social commitments from the companies they support.
